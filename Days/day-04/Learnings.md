# Connect a browser wallet

In the previous days, we graduated from generating a keypair manually -> persistent wallet using on-disk stored keypair. But that's not how normal users uses wallet and also it's quite cumbersome.

Think of google, all these different apps on internet asks you to sign in with your Google account. Google manages your identity and allows you to sign in to any app that supports Google Sign-In only exposing the bare minimum of your personal data. You don't share your password with any app, instead you delegate the authentication to Google. And Google on your behalf now grant access to scoped information to these apps.
